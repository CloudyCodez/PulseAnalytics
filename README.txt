Welcome! You're two clicks away from having Pulse running.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  FIRST TIME SETUP (do this once)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Double-click:  setup.bat

  The wizard will walk you through everything step by step.
  It takes about 10 minutes and opens each service for you.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  STARTING PULSE (after setup is done)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Double-click:  start.bat

  Pulse will open in your browser automatically.
  To stop Pulse, close the black window that appears.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  WHAT YOU'LL NEED FOR SETUP
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  The setup wizard will guide you through creating free
  accounts at these services:

    • Clerk      — handles your users' logins
    • Supabase   — your database
    • Stripe     — billing and subscriptions
    • Anthropic  — AI that writes your reports
    • Resend     — sends reports to your inbox
    • Inngest    — runs reports automatically every Monday

  Don't worry — the wizard opens each site for you and tells
  you exactly what to click and copy.
