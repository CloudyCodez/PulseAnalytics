Connect Google Analytics (GA4)
Sign in with Google to give Pulse read-only access to your GA4 analytics — website traffic, sessions, conversions, and more. Nothing is stored in the cloud, everything stays on this machine.

Setup · Step 3 of 4
Connect Shopify
Enter your Shopify store URL and Admin API access token. Create a read-only Custom App in Shopify Admin → Settings → Apps → Develop apps → give it read access to Orders, Products, and Customers.
